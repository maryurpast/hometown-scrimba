Top three places to visit in Vinnytsia

Light and Music Fountain Roshen

The largest floating fountain in Ukraine and Europe. It's length is 97m, width - 10m. Light and music water shows are held in the evenings, during the warm season every day.

Vinnytsia Water Tower

A very picturesque historic brick building 28 meters high standing on European Square. In the past, this water tower was also used as a fire tower.

Vinnytsia Museum of Local History

In this museum you can see artifacts from the burial ground of the Bronze Age, things of the Scythian and Sarmatian periods. There is also a large numismatic collection.
